1. Service Description

pipeline-dev ("the Service") is a content automation tool that reformats and publishes short-form gaming clip videos to TikTok on behalf of the authenticated account holder. The Service operates exclusively through the TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Eligibility

The Service is operated by a single developer for personal use. It is not offered as a public service or to third-party users. Only the developer's own TikTok account is used for posting.

3. Content

All clips posted through the Service originate from public live streaming platforms (Twitch, Kick). Only clips from streamers who have granted explicit written permission are processed and posted. The Service adds auto-generated captions and reformats clips to vertical video. Streamer credit is included in every post.

The Service does not collect, store, or share any TikTok user data. The only credential stored is the OAuth access token required to authenticate with the TikTok Content Posting API, which is stored locally on the developer's machine.
